Christy's Awesome Hot Ham and Cheese

This ham and cheese sandwich is great on a cool night with a cup of tomato soup. I add spinach, arugula, tomatoes, roasted peppers, chopped olives, artichokes, or a spoonful of salsa to the sandwich, depending on my mood and what's in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • 2 thin slices deli ham
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon whole grain mustard

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Spread one side of each slice of bread with 1 teaspoon butter.
  4. Place one slice butter-side down in the hot skillet. Top with Swiss cheese and ham.
  5. Spread the unbuttered side of second slice of bread with mayonnaise and mustard; place it butter-side up on sandwich.
  6. Cook in the hot skillet until golden brown and cheese is melted, about 3 minutes per side.
